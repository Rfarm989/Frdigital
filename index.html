Why Aren't You Showing Up on Google?

Usually it comes down to one of these six things. Here's how I fix each one.

Technical SEO

If Google can't properly find and read your site, nothing else matters. I find the technical problems quietly holding your rankings back, and I fix them.

On-Page SEO

A page that ranks is one built around what buyers type in when they're ready to hire. I shape your content, your layout, and your pages around that intent, so the right customers find you first.

Local SEO

When someone nearby searches for what you do, Google shows three local businesses before anything else. I work to make sure one of them is you.

Content & Strategy

Most businesses publish content without knowing if anyone's searching for it. I find the exact words and questions your customers Google before they hire someone, then build pages around those.

Link Building

Other websites linking to yours tells Google your business is credible. I build those connections carefully: earned links from real sources, nothing that could get your site penalized.

Reporting

You'll always know what I'm working on and whether it's working. Every month you get a plain-language report tied to rankings, traffic, and leads. Nothing buried in a dashboard you'll never open.
